Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enable certain functions of our dashboard
  • To provide analytics to understand how our dashboard is used
  • To remember your preferences

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems.

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our website.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.
