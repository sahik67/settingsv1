Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Device Monitor ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you may provide while using our application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to provide any personal information to use the App. However, the App collects information from your device as described below.

2.2 Device Information

We collect information about the device you install the App on, including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifier
  • Battery status
  • Network information

2.3 Activity Information

We collect information about activity on the device, including:

  • Location data
  • Call logs
  • SMS messages
  • App usage statistics
  • Web browsing history
  • Contacts
  • Photos and media (if enabled)
  • Recordings (if enabled)

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Store and display your monitored data
  • Communicate with you about updates or changes

4. Data Storage and Sharing

4.1 Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase and Cloudinary. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

4.2 Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating the App, such as cloud storage providers.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@devicemonitor.com
